\begin{abstract}
\begin{spacing}{1.5}
This template is modified from the undergraduate thesis LaTeX template of the College of Computer Science, Nankai University, and is temporarily used for undergraduate thesis writing of the College of Software. Since some formats of the LaTeX template from the College of Computer Science differ from the previous thesis format requirements of the College of Software, appropriate adjustments have been made. The latest version of the template from the College of Computer Science was updated in March 2024, and detailed information can be found on the official homepage: \text{https://tr0py.github.io/NKU-thesis-template-2020/}.

\end{spacing}
\end{abstract}
